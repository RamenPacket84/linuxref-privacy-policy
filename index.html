Privacy Policy for Terminal Atlas

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Terminal Atlas ("the app") is a digital command reference manual for iOS and iPadOS. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

Terminal Atlas does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and makes no network requests of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your bookmarked topics ("My Quick Refs")
  • Your selected theme and font size preference

This data never leaves your device. It is stored using iOS standard local storage (UserDefaults) and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Terminal Atlas uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external services of any kind.

Terminal Atlas does not collect data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future app update, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
